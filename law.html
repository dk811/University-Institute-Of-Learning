ABOUT COURSE

LLM is postgraduate Law course. Law is a rule or body of rules of conduct established and regulated by an authority. The duration of Master of Law is one year. Master of Law Degree is divided mostly into two semesters.

ELIGIBILITY & ADMISSION

LLB, BA-LLB, B.COM-LLB or BBA-LLB with 50% aggregate marks.

~Students will possess knowledge and understanding of substantive and procedural law.

~Demonstrate communication skills, including oral advocacy.

CAREER PROSPECTUS

Master of Law is an Internationally recognized Postgraduate Academic Master Degree which is awarded for a course or program in Law.LL.M. course structure is based upon various specialisations. LL.M. is especially meant for those candidates who want to specialize in a particular area of Law. It is also beneficial for those who wish to acquire in-depth knowledge in Law. It can also be pursued by those who are just not satisfies with a Bachelor’s Degree in the field of Law.Master of Law enables a candidate to gain specialized knowledge through research in a particular area of Law.An LL.M. degree enhances one’s repute as LL.M. degree signifies advanced, specialized legal training and it also signifies that a person is qualified to work in a multinational legal environment.
